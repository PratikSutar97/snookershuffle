How to use this app ?

  • This app is made for snooker shuffle game mode.

  • When a player qualifies by potting 2 red balls, he can press shuffle button and then he will get a random color as his color ball to pot and finish the game.

  • When all 6 colors are picked you can check colors picked in sequence order, players have to remember sequence of the players qualified and picked the color.

  • For checking colors tap on red heart below and then click on END GAME button and enter password - 1010
